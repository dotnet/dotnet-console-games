Connect 4 is a game where you try to get 4 pieces in a line before your oponent does on a 7x6 board by dropping them the pieces in from the top.

```
       v
   ╔---------------╗
   ║               ║
   ║               ║
   ║               ║
   ║               ║
   ║               ║
   ║               ║
   ║               ║
   ║   █   █       ║
   ║               ║
   ║ █ █   █ █     ║
   ║               ║
   ║ █ █   █ █   █ ║
   ╚═══════════════╝
```

## Input

- `←`, `→`: move your drop location
- `enter`: drop in your piece
- `escape`: exit game
